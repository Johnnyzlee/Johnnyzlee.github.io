Welcome to my homepage!

I am a first year Ph.D. student with the Department of Electronic and Computer Engineering (ECE), The Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (HKUST), Hong Kong. My advisor is Prof. Khaled B. Letaief.
Previously, I received my B.Eng. degree with honor in 2021 and my M.S. degree in 2024 from ShanghaiTech University, Shanghai, China, both in computer science, where I was advised by Prof. Yuanming Shi and (informally) by Prof. Dingzhu Wen.

Research Interest

I am interested in understanding, utilizing and developing elegant theoretical tools as well as provable learning-based methods to direct the design of and optimize the performance of the next generation communication networks. My particular focus lies in applying graph theory, discrete algorithms, and mathematical optimization for federated edge learning, computing power network and non-terrestrial networks in 5G/6G.
